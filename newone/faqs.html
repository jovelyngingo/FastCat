Boarding Procedure: (Phone Booking)

Be Sure to PRINT Accommodation Slip/Ticket which we send to you before going to the pier. (No Printed Accommodation Slip/Ticket – No Check In)

On the day of your schedule trip, PRESENT THE SAID ACCOMMODATION SLIP directly to the ticket counter of FAST CAT OUTLET at the pier (1) hour before your schedule departure time. NO NEED TO LINE UP anymore. At the COUNTER please ask for the following:

Bill of Lading (for those with vehicle) / Boarding Pass / Official Receipt if you desire

Then secure a Coast Guard Clearance for your Vehicle

After Which, You may now proceed to the PPA office for the following; (Please bring I.D.)

Terminal Fee / PPA Bill for your Vehicle

Then you may now proceed to the Boat.
